## Theory

Tokenization is the process of splitting text into smaller units called tokens such as words or sentences. It is the first step in Natural Language Processing (NLP). Tokenization helps machines understand and process textual data efficiently.

There are different types of tokenization:
- Whitespace Tokenization
- Punctuation-based Tokenization
- Treebank Tokenization
- Tweet Tokenization
- Multi-Word Expression (MWE) Tokenization

Stemming is the process of reducing words to their root form by removing suffixes.

Example:
playing → play

Common stemmers:
- Porter Stemmer
- Snowball Stemmer

Lemmatization converts words into meaningful dictionary base forms called lemmas.

Example:
running → run

NLTK library is used for tokenization, stemming and lemmatization in Python.
